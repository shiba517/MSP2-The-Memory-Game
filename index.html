the memory game

how to play

1. Match the yellow cards with the cards from below it in the order it is shown within the time limit. Sounds too easy?
2. But here's the tricky part; You will be given a short time to memorise where the matching cards are before they become invisible
3. The deeper you get into the game, the shorter time you will be given to memorise the cards; but you will be rewarded with more points.
4. The game will end once you run out of the lives
